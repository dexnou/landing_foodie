Food Delivery Day 2026 is a professional event focused on the delivery and digital gastronomy ecosystem in Latin America. It will take place on March 11, 2026, at Jano's Costanera in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

The event serves as a meeting point for restaurant owners, logistics managers, and technology platform leaders involved in the food delivery industry. Food Delivery Day 2026 emphasizes high-level networking and the analysis of key industry trends, including last-mile optimization, the growth of dark kitchens, and profitability strategies within delivery applications. The event provides a structured space to exchange knowledge and understand the current and future challenges of digital food delivery in the region.
